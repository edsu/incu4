Qualitative Coding

What is Qualitative Analysis?

A set of methods for developing models that allow you to answer questions about human experience.

Why?


  • Why are people communicating with @blklivesmatter?
  • Why is @blklivesmatter retweeting other accounts?
  • Why are activists groups using Twitter?

How?


  • How is @blklivesmatter using Twitter?
  • How are protests being coordinated on Twitter?
  • How do other activists groups interact with @blklivesmatter?

NVivo

  • Code: themes, concepts, ideas
  • Case: people, places, things
  • Annotation: notes about specific content
  • Memo: document emergent themes
  • Visualization: explore your data
